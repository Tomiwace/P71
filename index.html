New Updates

  • New School Canteen

    We can say our canteen is one of the best, it is always clean, no food fights and more. We have a variety of foods and we also have the lunch ladies make different foods so the children can test and be honest about if they like it or not and if they do, we put it on the menu. this is so that the children don't eat the same thing every day. We are also careful about what children are allergic to. We teach the children to be nice in the canteen, not to be too loud and to especially have no food fights so that they can be good amazing children when sitting around the table to eat with their family.

  • Online Attending Improved System

    Online Attending Improved System is specifically for children that go on their phones and go on social media or play games in school.
    This is a application that shows your child when they can call you and when they can not. We also put games that they can play only at break, lunch, and afternoon. But these games are quizzes based on their next class. This app also has a timetable.
    We have made this application so that children can behave better, so that they become smarter and be top students wherever they go. We want to give all our students an amazing future in their life. We do not want them to be sitting on their phones all day but we want them to strive to be the best in any class thay have.
